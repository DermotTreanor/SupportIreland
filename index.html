In-person support groups are a proven and effective way to tackle a wide range of mental health challenges. We naturally benefit from human care and support. By meeting with peers who are going through similar struggles you can all work together to make the improvements necessary for an important and fulfilling change.
It's natural to be anxious at the idea of meeting a group of new people to discuss such personal topics. We encourage you to push past that hesitation and reach out for peer support. Be curious and excited about the thought of some genuine help that is available; in time the anxiety will go!

When a support group is attended consistently it can return an immense benefit. It is with this in mind that we have collated a number of the best, in-person support options across the country. Too much information has been hidden away in specific websites or on pamphlets on clinic shelves. We hope that the information on this site will enable you to reach out to get some help.
