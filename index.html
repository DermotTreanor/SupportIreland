Find free and in-person support groups in your area.

In-person support groups are a proven and effective way to tackle a wide range of mental health challenges. We naturally benefit from human care and support. By meeting with peers who are going through similar struggles you can all work together to make the improvements necessary for an important and fulfilling change.

It's natural to be anxious at the idea of meeting a group of new people to discuss such personal topics. We encourage you to push past that hesitation and reach out for peer support. Try to be curious and excited about this unique and genuinely helpful option at your fingertips - in time the anxiety will go!

When a support group is attended consistently it can return an immense benefit. It is with this in mind that we have collated a number of the best, in-person support options across the country. Too much information had been hidden away in unknown websites or in pamphlets that were sitting on clinic shelves. We hope that the information assembled on this site will enable you to reach out to any and all groups that could be applicable.
